- The blacksmith Dummâll's turn against Nrírhkü in the tower:
  Dummâll was long the personal blacksmith of the Great Magician, and he was tasked to produce a magic sword and shield that could consume and deal out the blazing power of dragons. He turned against the Magician's counsellor, Nrírhkü, in his dragon form, wielding the commissioned arms, but could not overcome him. In a final act of desperation, he absorped the greatest blast of Nrírhkü's fire with the sword, stabbed it into the floor of the Magician's tower, and burned out the entire tower. Nrírhkü was lightly wounded, and the Great Magician lost many powerful artifacts to the blaze, but the war otherwise continued as before.
