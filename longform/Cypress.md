# Outline

## Beginning
- Cypress lived outside Monticello, FL, visiting grandparents in Dowling Park, FL
- Father works a blueberry farm
- Homeschooled w/ older sister by mother
- Sister ran away from home months ago
- Sees the woods passing Greenville with grandpa, pondering
- Cypress meets Anath in disguise at Brewer Lake
- Anath goes by "Al" ("Alaric"), incredibly old
- Al claims to know how to find his sister
- Several interactions, Al gaining trust
- Al demonstrates magic
- Tells Cypress
	- He comes from another world
	- His sister is probably in that world
	- Needs to use a vial of special water to get back
- Gives Cypress magical vial to stop the car at the right place
- Cypress uses it, wanders into the woods
- Vial glows, Cypress falls into strange pool

## Middle
- Cypress emerges in Teretz, near lake in Umethra
- Flees from strange creatures (like Enteledonts), drops vial
- Wanders the strange forest
- Found by elf
- Unable to understand, takes enchanted language berries
- Begin tales, general backstory
- Cypress moves into Umimdrí to search for sister
- Tours magical sites and experiences, learns about the world
- Hears the tale of the unnamed youth from earth who fought at Drithu
- No sign of sister, remembers missing vial
- Returns to woods with escort to find vial
- Vial found, water poured into pool
- Companion sees vial, works out Anath's identity and plan
- Sends Cypress back straightaway, to refuse Anath news of pool

## End
- Cypress returns through pool
- Confrontation with Anath waiting for him
- Anath needed to deposit water from Druthu to return to Teretz, attacks 
- Cypress uses elvish dagger surprise to escape
- Anath catches up by at interstate, promises to rescue sister and make Cypress a king
- Fight leads to car accident
- Cypress awakes in hospital with parents
